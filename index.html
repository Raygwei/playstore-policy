Privacy Policy for NSN: Learn to Read & Speak

Effective date: June 12, 2025

NSN ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your information when you use our mobile application, NSN: Learn to Read & Speak ("the App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Personal Data: We only collect personal information you voluntarily provide, such as:

  • Name or email address (if provided for account creation or feedback).
  • Any other information you submit through in-app forms or interactions.

Device and Usage Data: We collect anonymized data automatically, including:

  • Device Identifiers: Such as Advertising ID or IP address, collected via Google AdMob for ad personalization.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the App (e.g., pages viewed, time spent), collected for analytics and improvement.

Device Permissions: The App requests the following permissions, used only for specific features:

  • Microphone: To enable voice input for language learning features, used only with your consent.
  • Storage: To read and save files necessary for App functionality (e.g., lesson progress).
  • Bluetooth: To connect with nearby devices for specific features, used only with your consent.

Third-Party Data: We use Google AdMob to serve ads, which may collect anonymized data (e.g., Advertising ID, app usage) to provide personalized ads. See Google’s Privacy Policy for details.

We use the collected information to:

  • Deliver personalized ads through Google AdMob.
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations, such as responding to legal requests.

Data collected for ads is used only with your consent, obtained through in-app prompts or device settings.

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data as follows:

  • For Legal Reasons: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest.
  • Secure storage on protected servers.
  • Regular security audits to identify and address vulnerabilities.

Despite these measures, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy (e.g., to provide App functionality or comply with legal obligations). If you request deletion of your data, we will remove it from our systems within 30 days, unless required by law to retain it. To request deletion, contact us at n.raymondg2@gmail.com.
